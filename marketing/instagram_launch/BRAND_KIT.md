# NexRide — Social brand tokens

## Colour

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|--------|
| Black (primary bg) | `#0A0A0A` | Post/story backgrounds, depth |
| Black (lift) | `#141210` | Subtle gradients, cards |
| Gold (primary) | `#D4AF37` | Headlines, dividers, CTAs, logo accent |
| Gold (soft) | `#B8962E` | Secondary metallic tone |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Primary type on dark |
| Off-white | `#F5F0E6` | Subtle warmth on headings |

## Type (in SVG)

Use **SF Pro / Inter / Plus Jakarta Sans / Satoshi** in final exports if you substitute outlines. Kit SVGs default to **system-ui** stack for portability.

## Tone

Confident, calm, respectful. Mobility as **access and dignity**, not hype. Avoid clutter; **one headline + one subline** per frame.

## Motifs

Thin **gold horizontal rules**, **subtle radial glow** behind logo area, occasional **corner bracket** framing (premium, editorial).
